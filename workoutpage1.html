Workout Session

Push-Ups

Push-ups are a great upper body exercise that target the chest, shoulders, and triceps. Start in a plank position with your arms straight, then lower your body until your chest is close to the ground. Push back up to the starting position.

Squats

Squats are a fundamental lower body exercise. Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart, lower your body by bending your knees and pushing your hips back as if sitting in a chair. Return to the starting position.

Plank

The plank is a core-strengthening exercise. Begin in a push-up position but with your weight supported on your forearms. Keep your body in a straight line from head to heels and hold this position.

Lunges

Lunges target the quadriceps, hamstrings, glutes, and calves. Stand with your feet together, take a step forward with one leg, and lower your body until both knees are bent at a 90-degree angle. Return to the starting position and repeat with the other leg.

Crunches

Crunches are an effective abdominal exercise. Lie on your back with your knees bent and feet flat on the floor. Cross your arms over your chest and lift your upper body towards your knees. Lower back down and repeat.

Plank Shoulder Taps

Description: Plank shoulder taps are a core-strengthening exercise that also engage the shoulders and stabilizing muscles. Start in a plank position with your arms straight. Lift one hand off the ground and tap it on the opposite shoulder. Alternate sides while keeping your core engaged and minimizing hip movement.

Dumbbell Lunges

Dumbbell lunges are a variation of the standard lunge that add resistance to the exercise. Hold a dumbbell in each hand and stand with your feet together. Step forward with one leg and lower your body until both knees are bent at a 90-degree angle. Push back up to the starting position and repeat with the other leg.
